Seven wonders of the world

In Peru there is one of the seven new wonders of the world. In addition to being one of the most sought after and visited tourist spots in Peru, Machu Picchu is also known for being the sacred city of the Incas. Founded around the 15th century, the city was discovered in mid-1911 by the American anthropologist Hiran Bingham, who went to the country to research and study its different cultures and customs.

The oldest civilization

In Peru, there is not only the legacy and culture of the Inca civilization, the largest empire in pre-Columbian America. According to historians and researchers, the country was the land of the oldest civilization found in America. This civilization, known as Caral, developed between 3000 and 1700 BC.
